BCE (binary cross entropy)

Binary cross entropy:
$$
J(\theta) = - \dfrac{1}{m} \sum ^{m}{i=0} y{i} \log h(x_{i}; \theta) + (1-y_{i})\log (1 - h(x_{i}; \theta))
$$
where $x_{i}$ is the label and $y_{i}$ is input (image) of discriminator, therefore $y_{i}$

    1. generator loss
      For the generator, the loss is calculated by maximizing the critic‚Äôs prediction on the generator‚Äôs fake images. The argument has the scores for all fake images in the batch, but you will use the mean of them.

      For the critic, the loss is calculated by maximizing the distance between the critic‚Äôs predictions on the real images and the predictions on the fake images while also adding a gradient penalty.
